Secure Communication

All data transmission to Artivatic Products and services are encrypted using TLS 1.2 protocols, and we use certificates issued by SHA 256 based CA ensuring that our users have a secure connection from their browsers to our service. We use the latest and strong ciphers like AES_CBC/AES_GCM 256 bit/128 bit keys for encryption, SHA2 for message authentication and ECDHE_RSA as the key exchange mechanism.

Virus Scanning

Traffic coming into Artivatic (on AWS & Google Cloud) Servers is automatically scanned for harmful viruses using state of the art virus scanning protocols which are updated regularly.
